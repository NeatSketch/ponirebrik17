Ponirebrik is a St Petersburg-based convention for fans of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic TV show. First held on January 31, 2016, the event was attended by over 600 people.

Today we are proud to announce Ponirebrik 2017! The convention will take place on April 1-2, with the theme of the event being the Discord's Day.

Location: Tambovskaya ul., 63, Saint-Petersburg.

Featuring cosplay shows, musical performances, workshops, merchandise and entertainment booths, interviews with foreign stars and other acts, the program is made to impress.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or simply want to lend us a hoof, contact us at mail@ponirebrik.ru, and we will get in touch with you.
