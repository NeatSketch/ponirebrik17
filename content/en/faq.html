Guests

When and where will the event take place?
The event will take place on April 1-2 in St Petersburg, Russia. Exact time and location will be announced at a later date.

How much are the tickets and where do I buy them?
General admission ticket is 850 rubles for the first day and 150 rubles for the second day. Sponsors' tickets are 2000, 4000 and 8000 rubles. See the Tickets page for details on ticket types and purchase methods.

Can I purchase a ticket at the location? Will it cost me more?
Yes, you can, except for the sponsors' ones. They will cost a bit more. Get yours in advance!

What's the minimum age for attendance?
The event's age rating is 14+. If you are 13 or younger, you'll have to be accompanied by an adult. Don't worry that they'll get bored, we've got something for everyone.

Do I have to carry an ID?
No, but you probably should. You will need it if you're planning to purchase alcoholic drinks.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?
Only during the first day, not the second one. Please see the rules for details.

Singing Competition Participants

Do I have to submit a demo recording? Can I apply without it?
Yes, a demo recording is required. Applications without a demo recording will be declined.

What are the requirements for the demo recording?
The most important requirement is that your voice should be clear. Please note: your recording must not be altered via Autotune or similar software.

Can I play an instrument during my performance?
Probably yes.

When is the deadline for applications?
About 2-3 weeks before the event. More information will be available at a later date.

Can I send an application first, and the backing track later?
Yes, you can.

How much is the Singing Competition Participant's ticket?
Approved participants get a 20 % discount for general admission and early entrance tickets.

Will there be time for rehearsals?
Yes, there probably will be a small rehearsal on the day of the event. It's in your best interest to come earlier and get it over with.

What should I bring on the day of the event?
Take a copy of your backing track on a USB drive or any other media, just in case.

Can contest participants with costumes use the dressing rooms?
Yes.

Cosplayers

How can I participate in the cosplay show?
Fill out the form and apply for the contest.

Is there an age limit?
No.

What's the duration of an act?
1 minute for a solo act. 2-3 minutes for a group act.

Can I use a video during my act?
Yes. The video should be merged with your backing track into a single file.

What are the content quality requirements?
Music: mp3, 320 Kbit/s, Video: Full HD 1920x1080

Stage size? Wing flats? Number of entrances?
19,23х8,50 meters, with wings. Two entrances.

How much is the cosplayer's ticket?
Approved cosplayers participating in the show are admitted for free. Non-participants get a 30 % discount for general admission and early entrance.

Is the cosplayer's ticket good for both days?
Yes, if you wear a costume both days.

If I didn't apply as a cosplayer in advance, can I take advantage of the discount by showing my cosplay at registration?
No.

When is the deadline for cosplay applications?
Applications are submitted up to March 1, 2017.

Will there be dressing rooms available?
Yes, for approved cosplayers.

Are the dressing rooms separate?
No, there is a single large dressing room with cubicles for dressing up. The cubicles will have privacy screens.

Can I apply for the competition with several costumes?
Yes.

Can I be an extra for another cosplayer's act?
Yes, you can. But only the main cosplayer will be judged.

How many people can participate in a single act?
As many as you can fit on a stage (the only limit is your common sense).

Can I apply with an act or a costume that has already been presented on another event?
Yes, you can.

Media

What is required for accreditation?
Passion for work, a camera, good mood and a properly filled out and submitted application.

Are there any special requirements?
Of course there are, but they are pretty specific.

  • Photographers must have event photography and photo editing experience. Also they have to submit a portfolio with their application.
  • Videographers must have video equipment operation and video editing experience and have to submit 4 or more their works with their application.
  • Bloggers must submit links to their groups and channels with at least 10 thousand subscribers, provide examples of previous reviews of similar events, and describe their goal for attending the event.

Will I be allowed into the dressing room?
No. If you will need somewhere to store equipment in, mention it in the application or directly to the organizers in advance.

What are the terms and conditions of delivering the footage and reports?
Photographers: at least 200 photos, edited, with the event's watermark, within two weeks after the event.
Videographers: an edited video at least 3 minutes long, within one month after the event.
Bloggers: a report with photo/video content, published in their groups/channels, mentioning the event and featuring links to it, and/or a written report at least 300 words long, within two weeks after the event.

Additional requirements for publications?
The initial release of the photo and video content is made by the organizers as a single album, with references to the author.
All publications outside the organizers' site must include the name of the event and links to it.
