Equestria is, probably, one of the most musical countries. There are ponies constantly singing all over the shop. Ponyburg has to keep up, and that's why Ponirebrik is hosting a singing competition, where you can show off your singing talents. There are honorary prizes for the winners and consolation prizes for the rest.
